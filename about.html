Project of the month
Zipline: drones that save lives

One of start-ups that we are really interested in is called Zipline, it is the world’s only drone delivery system at national scale, such that it is able to to send urgent medicines to patients, regardless of their location. We think it puts an already created technology (drones) to a more altruistic purpose without having to build on the concept too much. Using drones bypasses so many of the issues that travel brings, and is much more time-sensitive option. Other than the innovation behind the project, We are impressed by the way the technology was altered to make ordering supplies accessible to the general public-- the start up allows for orders via text message and packs the orders within minutes in a centralised distribution centre.
